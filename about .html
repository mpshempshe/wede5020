Located in the vibrant township of Mankweng, Mankweng Auto Yard stands as a beacon for car enthusiasts and buyers seeking top-quality vehicles. Established in 2009 by the visionary duo of Thereso Maloleka and Lesedi Phashoko, this scrapyard has become a trusted name in the automobile market. Specializing in sought-after German, American, and Japanese cars, Mankweng Auto Yard caters to a diverse clientele with a passion for reliability, innovation, and style.

Over the past two years alone, the business has reached an impressive milestone, generating a remarkable R2 million in revenue. This achievement is a testament to the owners' dedication, market insight, and commitment to excellence. From luxury German engineering to cutting-edge Japanese designs and rugged American builds, Mankweng Auto Yard has curated an unmatched selection of vehicles that meets the needs and aspirations of every customer.

Beyond its financial success, Mankweng Auto Yard has played a pivotal role in fostering community growth, creating jobs, and supporting local businesses.
